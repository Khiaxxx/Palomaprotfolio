About Me

Hello! I’m Hezekiah Mae O. Paloma, a passionate and dedicated Frontend Developer and BSIT student from Cebu Technological University. I’ve always been fascinated by technology, creativity, and the idea of building something that people can interact with. Designing interfaces, solving problems, and creating smooth user experiences have become my way of expressing both logic and art.

I believe that learning never stops. Every project, every challenge, and every mistake pushes me to grow—not just as a developer, but as a person. My goal is to become a skilled professional who can contribute meaningful solutions to real-world problems, especially in the digital world.


Vision

To become a highly skilled and innovative IT professional who designs impactful and user-centered digital experiences. I envision myself contributing to tech communities, building projects that inspire others, and continuously learning as technology evolves.


Mission

• To collaborate with people and bring creative ideas to life.
• To use technology as a tool to help others and make everyday tasks easier.
• To stay curious, passionate, and resilient in every challenge I face.


Core Values

• Innovation: I aim to create solutions that make a difference.
