Web App Name:

JUNKY TRADE SHOP


Junky Trade Shop is an online junk shop platform with three types of users: User, Employee, and Admin.


1. User Role

Users can sell items by uploading a picture, a short description, the item name, and their desired price. To prevent scams, they are also required to upload an MP4 video showing themselves holding or carrying the actual item. After submitting the item details, the user can choose whether they want to personally deliver the product to the shop or have the shop pick it up, which includes an additional fee.


2.Employee Role

Employees are responsible for verifying the user’s submission. They will check if the MP4 video matches the photo and description. Once the product arrives at the shop, the employee confirms whether it is the same item shown in the video. If everything is accurate, the employee approves the item and lists it in the shop so that it becomes visible to buyers.


3.Admin Role

Administrators manage employee accounts, monitor employee activity (such as online status), and can access user accounts for handling reports or appeals. Admins also have the authority to edit or remove products that are inappropriate or mistakenly approved during verification.
